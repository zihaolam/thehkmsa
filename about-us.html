About Us

HKMSA

Hong Kong Malaysian Student Association (HKMSA) is a non profit student organisation founded in 2019 with the aim of uniting and connecting Malaysian students, employers and organisations in Hong Kong. HKMSA strives to provide support and enhance the welfare of Malaysian students through various ways and organize events for you to build networks with fellow Malaysians from different universities.

We sincerely hope that this association would serve as a meaningful and beneficial platform throughout your journey in Hong Kong.

GOALS AND MISSIONS

-Unite Malaysian students in HK

-Connect Malaysian students, employers and organisations

-Stimulate tight bonds and build long lasting friendships

-Enhance the welfare of Malaysian students

-Share our beautiful Malaysian culture
